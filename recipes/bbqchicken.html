Crunchy Onion BBQ Chicken

Description

Crispy onions and tangy-sweet BBQ sauce create a perfect topping of texture and flavor for these chicken tenders!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 1/3 cup crushed french-fried onions
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 4 chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Put BBQ sauce in a shallow bowl, and onions, cheese, pepper in a separate bowl.
  3. Dip chicken in BBQ sauce, then dredge through the onion mixture.
  4. Place chicken in sprayed baking pan.
  5. Bake for 22-27 minutes or until the juices run clear.
