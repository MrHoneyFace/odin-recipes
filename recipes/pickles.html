Easy Refrigerator Pickles

Description

Preserve your home-grown cucumbers or use cucumbers bought from your local grocery store - either one will yield delicious, sweet-tangy pickles in just a few easy steps!

Ingredients

  • approx. 2 lbs cucumbers
  • 2 cups thinly sliced onion
  • 1 1/2 cups white vinegar
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp. celery seeds
  • 1/2 tsp. turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Place cucumbers and onions in bowls, jars, etc.
  2. Combine all remaining ingredients in a saucepan.
  3. Bring to a boil, and continue boil for 1 minute.
  4. Pour over cucumbers and let cool.
  5. Cover and chill at least 4 days before enjoying.
