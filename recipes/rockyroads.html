Rocky Roads

Description

Graham crackers topped with nuts, chocolate chips, and marshmallows make for a delicious treat any time!

Ingredients

  • 12 double graham crackers
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 6 oz. chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup salted peanuts
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Arrange crackers in a single layer in a jelly roll pan.
  3. Sprinkle with peanuts, chocolate chips, and marshmallows.
  4. In a small saucepan, combine butter and sugar.
  5. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar has dissolved.
  6. Add vanilla. Drizzle evenly over prepared graham crackers.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until marshmallows are melted and slightly browned.
  8. Cool on wire rack. Cut into small bars, following perforations in crackers. Store covered in fridge up to 2 days or wrap and freeze up to one month.
