Soft Sandwich Bread

Description

This soft sandwich bread recipe will have your family clamoring for more! Perfect for sandwiches, toast, or eaten with simple butter or jam, this bread is guaranteed to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm water
  • 12 1/2 tsp. yeast
  • 1/2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 5-7 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Combine yeast, sugar, and water in mixing bowl; let sit 10 minutes.
  2. Add remaining ingredients except the flour, and mix.
  3. Add 3 cups flour and use dough hook to mix.
  4. Add 1 cup of flour at a time, kneading well after each cup. After 5 total cups of flour, add in 1/2 cup at a time until dough is no longer sticking to the sides of the bowl.
  5. Knead for about 10 minutes longer.
  6. Cover dough and sides of bowl with shortening; cover with towel and let rise about 1 to 1.5 hours.
  7. Punch down and dump on floured surface. Knead for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Divide dough into 2 parts and roll into flat rectangles. Roll up each rectangle and tuck ends under; place into 2 greased loaf pans.
  9. Let rise again for about 30-45 minutes.
  10. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  11. After rising, bake loaves for 25 minutes or until golden brown. Brush tops with melted butter, remove bread from pans, and cool completely before storing.
